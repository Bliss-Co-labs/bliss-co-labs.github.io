About Us

At Navotpala Tech, we bring together a seasoned team with a rich history of pioneering advancements in Android and Linux technologies.  We pride ourselves on our dual commitment to corporate innovation and open source collaboration. By harnessing the collective expertise of our team and the vibrant contributions of the open source community, we deliver innovative development solutions that redefine technological excellence.

Our core values are part of the value added proposition we bring to the table when we work with customers.

  • Innovation
  • Accountability
